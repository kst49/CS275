Fibonacci Numbers

Leonardo Fibonacci

Fibonacci numbers, commonly denoted Fn is one of the most famous formulas in mathematics. Each number in the sequence is the sum of the two numbers that precede it, starting from 0 and 1. The sequence goes like this: 0, 1, 1, 2, 3, 5, 8, 13, and so on.That is

F0 = 0, F1= 1, and Fn = Fn-1 + Fn-2, for n>1.
